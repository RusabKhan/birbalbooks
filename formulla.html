A formulla that lets you search your desired record by name. Type "N" on your search bar and press tab to autocomplete then between the colon write the name of the recods you want to search then press enter. The example below will result in the ledger fetching all recodss named as hammer.
A formulla that lets you search your desired record by the detail you entered. Type "detail" on your search bar and press tab to autocomplete then between the colon write the detail by which you want to search. In the below example all the records with the detail of "Garage Sale" will be fetched
A formulla that lets you search your desired record by the Transaction ID you entered. Type "ID" on your search bar and press tab to autocomplete then between the colon write the detail by which you want to search. In the below example the record with Transaction Id of "1098765-01" will be fetched
A formulla that lets you search your desired record by date. Type "d" on your search bar and press tab to autocomplete then between the colon write the starting date and ending date and press enter. In the below example the ledger will fetch all the records from january to february.
A formulla that lets you search your desired record by their count(Number of recodss). Type "C" on your search bar and press tab to autocomplete then between the colon write the starting count and ending count and press enter. In the below example the ledger will fetch all the records from which were entered in the count range from 200 to 300.
A formulla that lets you search your desired record by their Rat. Type "R" on your search bar and press tab to autocomplete then between the colon write the starting count and ending count and press enter. In the below example the ledger will fetch all the records from which were entered in the rate range from 100 to 300.
A formulla that lets you search your desired record by their End Balance. Type "B" on your search bar and press tab to autocomplete then between the colon write the starting count and ending count and press enter. In the below example the ledger will fetch all the records from which were entered in the end balance range from 3000 to 20000.
AND lets you conjoin two or more formullas together such as NAME LIKE:: and DATE :: TO ::. Here we have a normal Name Like formulla. when we write A after the formulla, the Formulla Bar starts suggesting us that we are trying to conjoin the formullas by using And. Press tab and it will write and itself. After adding AND we can now write the date formulla just normally. Type d and press tab. Here the formulla has been completed for us. It will display all records with the recods name "Hammer" between the months of January and February. Just like that we can combine as many formullas together as we want.
