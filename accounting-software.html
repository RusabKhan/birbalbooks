EXCEL SOFTWARE FOR ACCOUNTING

  1. Home
  2. EXCEL SOFTWARE FOR ACCOUNTING

In the last two decades, Pakistani businesses have witnessed a rapid change in the traditional way of management. From restaurants turning towards Point Of Sales and enterprises moving towards ERP solutions, shopkeepers and mid-level businessmen are trying to find a solution of their own. Some of them turn towards Sheet Apps and some of them prefer Online Spreadsheets but these applications require management of their own.

We provide a solution which not only combines an online spreadsheet with a daily expense or salary sheet or even a ledger, It provides nearly all functionality which a business asks from an excel software for accounting. Whether it is bookkeeping accounting or accounting ledger our solution ensures that the user gets all needed functionality with no extra management or time consumption for their business. While computer software for accounting does seem like a good idea its limitation on mobile devices is a big problem. Most Pakistani businessmen travel for their work if not from city to city then from shop to shop hence the complexity of the problem of computer software. Even if a software is available online what of the mobile data? That would become an additional cost which is why we have specifically designed our ledger to work differently on mobile and save as much data as possible. A whole day of record insertion is at max going to take the same amount of data as watching a single low-quality youtube video and this makes it an ideal companion.

Another problem which most Pakistani businesses face in terms of modern-day technology is being less than fluent with the English language. So being a Pakistani company we have taken a step forward to understand and acknowledge this need, We answer emails in both English and Urdu. We also allow our users to contact us in SMS format English(Roman English). We take care of our quality and your satisfaction is the measurement of how much quality we have.

We help businesses in Pakistan grow by taking away their need for manual calculations and manual finance plotting. Manual calculations are bound to be wrong now and then and for businesses one wrongdoing means a loss in your bank balance hence let us do this for you. With automated calculations the precision in data is clear and through features like graphical representation of profit/loss statement, suggestion feature, and search function for records our ledger becomes your ideal companion.
