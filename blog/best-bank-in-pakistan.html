Top 5 Banks In Pakistan

Posted by Birbal Books

Banks are an integeral part of a countries economy. They provide businesses with much needed resources such as loans, safes and much more. Choosing the wrong bank can lead to a buisness losing tons of money and top of that wasting time. Here are our top 5 picks for the best banks in Pakistan.

Habib Bank Limited - HBL

HBL is the most popular bank in Pakistan. With well over 11,000 ATMs providing 24/7 cash withdrawl services to its customer, HBL easily becomes the ultimate bank when it comes to making quick transaction. HBL also has made significant effort to provide more digital support to its customers through the HBL mobile application. You can now easily inquire about your bank balance or send amount using your cellphone, To provide proper secuirty HBL also makes sure to let you know whenever any event occurs in your HBL mobile app via SMS or Email.

All of these feature plus great customer support and timely responses puts HBL on the top list for the best bank in Pakistan.

National Bank Of Pakistan - NBP

National Bank Of Pakistan belongs to the goverment of Pakistan and has been functioning since 1949. Although being a goverment department does affects its reputation, NBP provide more than 1000 ATMs and 1450 branches all across the country. NBP also has representative offices in foreign countries such as Canada and China making it an ideal option for banking for Chinese or Canadian Pakisanis while doing its best to have a digital footprint. Its headquarter is located in the financial capital of Pakistan, Karachi

Meezan Bank

Meezan is one of the first Islamic Banks. Established in 1947 Meezan is also one of the oldest Pakistani Bank. It is controlled by a Kuwaiti company Noor Financial Investments. Meezan has its headquarters in Karachi and provides a well of 760 branches all across the country. Being a Muslim Bank and working according to islamic law, Meezan provides interest free schemes such as Car Ijarah. The financial product of Meezan are based on leasing.

MCB

Founded in 1947 MCB is one of the oldest and biggest names in banking in Pakistan. It serves an estimate of 40 Lakh Pakistanis and have nearly 1100 branches all across the country and 8 branches globally. MCB has provided employment to 14,000 Pakistanis. In the year 2005 the MCB administration shortened its title to MCB from Muslim Commercial Bank in order to avoid work permits from western countries. MCB also claims to be the largest bank of Pakistan in terms of internet banking.

United Bank Limited - UBL

UBL is one of the largest commerciall bank. It is controlled by a British company Bestway Group and its headquarter is based in Karachi. UBL claims to have 35 Lakh customers and provides 1380 branches all across Pakistan and 19 branches globally. UBL provides its services in countries namely UAE, Bahrain, Qatar, China, Oman, Yemen, UK, Switzerland, the US, Tanzania, Iran, and Pakistan. It has billions of assets and a huge number of employees.
