Bookkeeping & Accounting

Bookkeeping accounting in Pakistani perspective normally refers to the maintaining and writing of a ledger, however, it is not that simple. Bookkeeping largely refers to the record management of any transactions occurring in a business or whatever scope that is to be recorded while ledger refers to the profit and loss of a business, or transaction in terms of monetary value. Bookkeeping is record management while ledger is record keeping.

While most Pakistani businesses still use a general ledger book for their record management many of them have moved forward and started using more modern techniques such as a spreadsheet or the ever most popular MS Excel. They are relatively more powerful and their data capacity is also exponentially better than that of an average ledger book. But even this is not enough for some businesses! Some businesses are taking the next step forward and are using online excel sheets or online spreadsheets for their record management. This not only allows them to be ahead of other business since they can carry their record everywhere with them by just having a cell phone in their pocket but also allows them to have this data shared easily with their partners hence the vast majority of businesses that follow these record management models are businesses that want faster record management while minimizing the cost. They want to be able to handle their record in a fashion that once they are done they should not be worried about ever losing them..

The number of businesses that still use old methodologies for record management is still in millions but they are slowly converting to more computerized methods, the reason being the fact that if you don’t convert to modern-day technology you will simply be left behind. Hence the larger corporations have moved to what is now known as an ERP.

Enterprise resource planning is a software that solves the hurdles of a business such as a salary management, inventory, profit/loss statement, product sales, daily expense sheet, and much more. It has quickly become one of the most essential tools for a large business that wants to grow even larger. This is why the market is currently heavy on sheet apps and small to large ERP’s but even with all its might, the cost of an ERP is something that not every business can afford.

Sheets App

We live in a world in which every single problem has multiple solutions to it and while some of those solutions would be ideal for a certain group, they would be disastrous to another. Choosing what is right for you is the way to go forward and here are some of our thoughts on what you should choose for your record-keeping needs.

ONLINE EXCEL SHEET

Businesses all over the world have turned towards online excel sheets to cover their record management needs. With tools like Auto-Filter, formula bar, graphs etcetera excel has quickly become a must-know tool for businesses. The excel spreadsheet is ideal for a businessman who has command over excel but what about those who don’t? Not all Pakistani business owners have proper command over excel but this does not mean that they are doomed to fail. There are different tools available that are simpler and just as effective as excel.

BOOKKEEPING SERVICES

There are some companies that offer bookkeeping services for small to large businesses their prices may vary depending on the work that you want to achieve from them. Some of them provide advice depending upon their experience while some of them provide tools that ease everyday hectics of bookkeeping. They may be somewhat similar to excel but have their unique abilities as a plus, which is the main reason why people purchase them. They can be called “excel software for accounting”.

ERP

ERPs are software that are used mostly by large businesses. They consist of powerful builtin tools that help the user keep track of their cash flow and calculations. Some advanced ERPs also provide features such as staff and inventory management while some ERPs even consist of project and supply chain management. Even with all these features their prices make them less than ideal for a small/medium business owner.
