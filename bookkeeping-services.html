With the market ever growing its boundaries of profit, the days where businessmen had time on their hands for accounting is far gone. Most businesses are referring to either accountant’s or have started using excel for accounting but an accountant can not always be available or beside you and excel is quite complex to understand for the average Pakistani business owner. This is where we come in.

Ledger & Spreadsheet

Excel provides a spreadsheet that is designed to provide a wide variety of functions and calculations for their users. We have trimmed the same experience down to just bookkeeping or khata management. We focus on the main hurdles of ledger maintenance and try to counter them so that you may have more time on your hand for your other ventures.

An online excel sheet is a lot more than ideal for general ledger use or record keeping. Its functionalities are made for a wider audience than businessmen. We on the other hand keep businessmen as our top priority. In an excel spreadsheet, you have to define all your calculation regarding your profit/loss and transactions while we calculate all of that for you as soon as you enter a single record the graphs and well as the automated inventory system all is pre-designed the only thing that you need to do is sign up and start using.

The online presence of our app is another benefit! You don’t need to carry a book in your hand for record-keeping anymore when you can instantly access an online sheet on your mobile phone. The app is also designed separately for mobile phones so that the user would get a customized feel for mobile. A general ledger does not give you the ability to search your records through a search bar while with our app you can easily navigate through your records depending upon their name, date, or even credit or debit. We also provide our users with a graph that will show them how much profit they are making on a certain item and how much profit they are making every month! This is to ensure that the user can make better business decisions for their future using a graphical representation of data.

In short the benefits of using our product include.

  • Automated calculation on each record entry.
  • Suggestion system for recurring records.
  • Graphical representation of the profit/loss statement per product and month.
  • Search function for records.
  • Mobile-friendly design.
  • Simplicity and easily understandable UI.
  • Unlimited lines and no additional hectic of sheet management.
