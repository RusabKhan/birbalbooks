About Ledger

Our double entry ledger provides you with automatic calculations of all your records reducing the chance of error to 0. It also consists of a search bar which can find records by the particular feature you want such as by name or date while our suggestion features make it easier for you to enter records. All of your record transactions are saved in a secure database & have unique id. All of this makes our ledger the most modern way of record management.


About Inventory

Inventory is a dynamic item management system. Foreach record you save in your inventory you will see the same data pop up in your ledger when adding records you can also search for these items, this will help you insert records faster and more accurately. Gone are the days when you needed to write the same thing over and over again!


About Graph

Our graph shows you your income and expenses through out the month and per item. All your ledgers data transformed into a bar graph so that you can view it easily and make better decisions.
